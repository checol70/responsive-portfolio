About Me

I am a beginning web developer. I love games and have been building my own for a couple of years. Slight disclaimer, no monitors were punched in the process of the games development (yet). Desks are another story. In the course of developing it I have found that I like the coding far more than any other part of game development. This has led me to learn web development.

I love coding and am learning fast. I hope to use my skills to gain employment so that I can raise a (hopefully) gaming family.
